Low-hanging fruit

A playful impact × effort matrix for picking delicious tasks

What is this

A tool to help us prioritize tasks (fruits), by placing them on a chart (tree):

  • Fruits at the top are harder to get (more effort)
  • Fruits to the right are tastier (more impact)

We should often pick fruits that are low effort and high impact (bottom-right).

How to use
  1. Tap green canopy to place tasks
  2. Label them (like “Launch product”)
  3. Drag more impactful tasks to the right
  4. Drag easier tasks to the bottom
  • Dislike a task? Drag it to the trash
  • Task is done? Drag it to the cart
  • Cart is full? Empty it
  • Start over? Clear all (can’t undo)
  • Hate trees? List tasks instead
